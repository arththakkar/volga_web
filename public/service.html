Services & Help


Schedule Booking

We know the real meaning of schedule booking that is why we give you the best feature of our app. If you book a schedule booking you will get driver detail at the same time and you can also confirm it by calling the driver. In case if your flight arrived late then don't worry our driver will wait for you. His utmost concern is to make sure that you reach your destination.

Before you confirm your booking you will get two fares, one is estimated fare and the other one is exact fare. Exact fare is for cashless trip(it must be paid either by card or by wallet). You can choose from any of them.  Read More..

You can choose your ride as follows

  • Small cab as current booking
  • Eeco cab as current booking
  • Suv as current booking as well as schedule booking
  • Comfort cab as current booking as well as schedule booking
  • Compact cab as schedule booking with boarding point
  • Sightseeing as schedule booking with boarding point
  • Rental booking as current booking as well as schedule booking (with fare starting from 2Rs/km launching offer)
  • 10% fare can be reduced if you pay using credit/debit card and wallet payment
We provide our cab services in areas where transport connectivity is very low but it is advisable to go for schedule booking.

Hail Booking

Volga cabs, introduces Hail booking service as an additional feature for its customers. This service ensures that passengers don’t ever miss a ride, even when they are not on internet for any reason. If they see a volga cab, they can hail it and book it, right there. It is for the first time ever that this feature is introduced in Ahmedabad. Passanger should hail the cab and and inform driver about the drop location and provide his/her mobile number to the driver. The driver will give a fare estimate to the passanger and will drop the passanger to destination.


Download App

Volga Cabs app is Ahmedabad's most popular taxi service app that links you to the city’s largest fleet of cabs – anytime, anywhere. Volga Cabs app saves time and money when requesting a cab. It’s ideal during peak periods, including weekends and special events and cutting through queues.

Booking a Cab with VolgaCabs app is very simple!

STEPS TO USE APP:
  • Make Sure that You Have Volga Cabs App Installed in Your Phone and have active Internet connection on your smartphone.
  • Open the App.
  • Ensure the smartphone could fetch the location or it is enabled.
  • Select Cabs Around You.
  • Wait for Confirmation Message.

The app can also estimate your fare, track your vehicle location and take payment for your ride.
